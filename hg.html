1.流体流动

  1. 理想气体方程：\(\rho =\cfrac{m}{V}=\cfrac{pM}{RT} \)
  2. \(1atm=101325Pa \approx 101.3kpa = 0.1013MPa = 760mmHg \approx 10.33mH_2O \)
  3. 表压=绝压-当地大气压
  4. 真空度=当地大气压-绝压
  5. \(U形压差管满足:p_1 - p_2 = (\rho_2 - \rho_1)gR \)
  6. \(实际流体的机械能衡算方程：\begin{cases}gz_1+\cfrac{u_1^2}{2}+\cfrac{p_1}{\rho}+w_e=gz_2+\cfrac{u_2^2}{2}+\cfrac{p_2}{\rho}+w_f\\\\z_1+\cfrac{u_1^2}{2}+\cfrac{p_1}{\rho g}+h_e=z_2+\cfrac{u_2^2}{2g}+\cfrac{p_2}{\rho g}+w_f\end{cases} \)
  7. \(若流体静止,则u=0,w_e=0,w_f=0,于是式子变为: gz_1+\cfrac{p_1}{\rho}=gz_2+\cfrac{p_2}{\rho} \)
  8. \(若流体系统无外加光,即w_e=0,则机械能衡算方程变为: gz_1 + \cfrac{u_1^2}{2}+\cfrac{p_1}{\rho}=gz_2+\cfrac{u_2^2}{2}+\cfrac{p_2}{\rho}+w_f \)
  9. \( 雷诺数:Re=\cfrac{du\rho}{\mu}\ Re \le 2000时属于层流,Re \gt 4000时属于湍流 \)
  10. \(单位质量流体的沿程损失 w_f=\lambda \cfrac{l}{d}\cdot \cfrac{u^2}{2}(J \cdot kg^{-1}) \)
  11. \(单位体积的流体的沿程损失 \Delta p_f=\rho w_f=\lambda \cfrac{l}{d} \cdot \cfrac{\rho u^2}{2} (J \cdot m^{-3} or Pa) \)
  12. \(单位重量流体的沿程损失 h_f=\cfrac{w_f}{g}=\lambda \cfrac{l}{d} \cdot \cfrac{u^2}{2g} \)
  13. \(层流时:\lambda = \cfrac{64}{Re} \)
  14. \(非圆形管的沿程损失计算要将管径d用d_e替换:d_e=4 \times 水力半径=\cfrac{4 \times 流通截面积}{湿润周边} \)
  15. 任一管路的总阻力损失等于所有沿程损失与局部阻力损失之和:

    \(w_f=\sum \left(\lambda \cfrac{l}{d}+\sum \xi \right)\cfrac{u^2}{2}=\sum \lambda \left(\cfrac{l+\sum {l_e}}{d}\right)\cfrac{u^2}{2} u=\cfrac{4V_s}{\pi d^2} \)
  16. \(测速管测速:v=\sqrt{\cfrac{2gR(\rho_0 - \rho)}{\rho}},若被测流体为气体可以忽略分子的"-\rho" \)
  17. \(孔板流量计:\begin{cases}u_0=C_0\sqrt{\cfrac{2(p_1 - p_0)}{\rho}}\\\\u_0=C_0\sqrt{\cfrac{2gR(\rho_0 - \rho)}{\rho}}\\\\ V_s=u_0 A_0\end{cases} \)

2.流体运送机械

  1. 离心泵叶轮分为：开式、半开式、闭式
  2. \(管路所需压头:h_e=\Delta z+ \cfrac{\Delta p}{\rho g} + \cfrac{\Delta u^2}{2g} + \sum h_f \)
  3. \(H = h_0 + \cfrac{p_c - p_b}{\rho g} + \cfrac{u_c^2 - u_b^2}{2g} + h_{f,b-c} \eta = \cfrac{N_e}{N} = \cfrac{HQ\rho g}{N} \)
  4. \( 比例定律(转速n): \begin{cases} \cfrac{Q'}{Q} = \cfrac{n'}{n}\\\\ \cfrac{H'}{H} = \left(\cfrac{n'}{n}\right)^2\\\\ \cfrac{N'}{N} = \left(\cfrac{n'}{n}\right)^3 \end{cases} \)
  5. \( 比例定律(叶轮尺寸D): \begin{cases} \cfrac{Q'}{Q} = \left(\cfrac{D'}{D}\right)^3\\\\ \cfrac{H'}{H} = \left(\cfrac{D'}{D}\right)^2\\\\ \cfrac{N'}{N} = \left(\cfrac{D'}{D}\right)^5 \end{cases} \)
  6. \( 管路特性方程: \begin{cases} h_e = \Delta z + \cfrac{\Delta p}{\rho g} + \cfrac{\Delta u^2}{2g} + \sum h_f\\\\ \sum h_f = \lambda \cfrac{\sum{l + l_e}}{d}\cdot \cfrac{u^2}{2g}=\left(\cfrac{8\lambda}{\pi ^2 g}\right)\cfrac{\sum(l + l_e)}{d^5}Q^2 \end{cases} \)
  7. \(汽蚀余量:\Delta h = \left(\cfrac{p_e}{\rho g} + \cfrac{u_e^2}{2g} \right)- \cfrac{p_v}{\rho g} \)

5.传热

  1. \(q=\cfrac{dQ}{dA} \)
  2. \(传热速率 = \cfrac{传热温差}{热阻} \)
  3. \(Q=\cfrac{t_1 - t_{n + 1}}{\sum _{i=1}^{n}\cfrac{b_i}{\lambda_i A_{mi}}} = \cfrac{t_1 - t_{n + 1}}{\sum _{i=1}^{n}\cfrac{\ln(r_{i + 1}/r_i)}{2 \pi l\lambda_i}} \)
